Optimise Your Built Asset Inspection and Management

We offer complete inspection and management solutions, with the flexibility to engage our services at any stage of the process.

Robotic inspection

Our advanced robotic inspection system transforms traditional manual processes through automated data collection. Using unmanned aircraft systems (UAS), we eliminate the need for dangerous manual access while capturing comprehensive high-quality data. Our platform enables precise, thorough inspections of hard-to-reach areas while minimizing site disruption.

Artificial Intelligence powered data analysis

Our artificial intelligence technology transforms raw inspection data into actionable insights. Trained on a comprehensive dataset of inspection reports, our AI automatically detects, segments, and precisely measures potential defects. This enables unprecedented accuracy in identifying and characterizing built asset conditions, from minor surface irregularities to significant structural concerns.

Digital Twin Platform for visualisation, multi-layer data management, and decision support

Our digital twin platform revolutionizes how inspection data is visualized and managed. By integrating multi-layer inspection data into a unified 3D environment, we enable interactive visualization and comprehensive asset tracking. The platform supports informed decision-making through precise damage mapping, historical data comparison, and seamless access to design and construction information.
